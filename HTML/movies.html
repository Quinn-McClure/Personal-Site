My Movie Reviewing: The Queue

The Queue has been a passion project of mine for close to two years now. Here I give my opinion on movies and TV shows I watch. I rate on a 10 point scale with 1 being bad and 10 being absolutely astounding and perfect. I judge based on cinematography, acting, story quality, and sound design. Something that sets me apart from other movie reviewers is that I consider myself preaching to the public, making reviews for the average movie goer. My main platforms are Instagram and Letterboxd although I am trying to diversify into YouTube. Please feel free to check out my page that has my dedicated reviews below or the other places I host my reviews!
